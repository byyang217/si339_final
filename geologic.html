The Climate of Earth Over Geologic Time

Earth has had some interesting climates overall for the first few billion years of it's existence. In the Hadean Era, there was no atmosphere, there was frequent volcanic activity, and an impact with a Mars-sized planet whose debris would form into the Moon. In the Archean Era, oceans and the oxygen-rich atmosphere formed. There are many periods of Snowball Earth, the earliest of which was around 2 billion years ago.
